

# Pulse Fitness — Modern Gym Website

## Brand & Design System
- **Dark theme** with black background, orange primary (#FF6B00), yellow accent (#FFD700) for CTAs
- Bold, athletic typography with large headings
- High-contrast orange/yellow buttons on dark surfaces
- Mobile-first responsive design with a premium, powerful feel
- Pulse Fitness logo embedded in the navbar and footer

---

## Page 1: Home
- **Hero Section** — Full-width dark hero with bold headline: *"Train Hard. Train Smart. Train at Pulse."* and orange/yellow CTA buttons ("Join Now" / "Book a Trial")
- **Intro Section** — Brief paragraph about Pulse Fitness's mission and philosophy
- **Programs Highlights** — 6 cards showcasing: HYROX Preparation, Group Training, Personal Training, CrossFit, Functional Training, Strength Training — each with icon, short text, and "Learn More" link
- **Testimonials** — Client quotes in a carousel or grid
- **Bottom CTA Banner** — Bold call-to-action strip encouraging sign-ups

## Page 2: Programs
- Dedicated sections for each program (HYROX Prep, HYROX Group Batches, Personal Training, CrossFit, Functional Training, Strength Training)
- Each with benefit-driven copy, strong heading, and CTA button
- Clean alternating layout for visual rhythm

## Page 3: About Us
- Gym philosophy, coaching approach, and "Why Choose Pulse Fitness"
- Focus on discipline, structure, and performance messaging
- Bold imagery placeholders and strong typography

## Page 4: Pricing
- Clean membership plan cards (e.g., 3 tiers)
- Best value plan highlighted with yellow accent border/badge
- CTA button on each plan

## Page 5: Contact
- Contact form with name, email, phone, message fields (validated with Zod)
- Phone number display and WhatsApp button (linking to WhatsApp API)
- Google Maps embed showing gym location
- Location address details

---

## Global Components
- **Sticky Navigation Bar** — Logo + links to all pages, mobile hamburger menu, prominent "Join Now" CTA button
- **Footer** — Contact info, social media links (Instagram, etc.), quick navigation links
- **Instagram Feed Section** — Embedded Instagram feed or styled placeholder grid on the home page
- **Strong CTA Buttons** — Consistent orange/yellow styling throughout all pages

## Tone & Copy
- Confident, direct, performance-driven language throughout
- No fluff — focused on results, strength, and transformation
